About the game

(Our game PROJECTION is curently in progress)

Projection is a 2D Infinte Level game, Or is it? In PROJECTION you navigate procedurally generated rooms fighting enimies spawning from a spawner, you must destroy the spawner in the room so you can stop the monsters from spawning THERE. You have a battery that is empty and through machines you can turn in documents for a bit of battery, Every time you hurt and enimie you gain Documents based on the attack and damage done, sometimes when you enter a room there could be a machine to fill up your battery you can not reuse the machines so you will need to find a new one. After your battery is filled you must find your way back but if you destroyed the spawners you will be safe walking back, you must reach the elevator you started in, turn in your battery, and get in the elevator, Then you will be entering the next level,
